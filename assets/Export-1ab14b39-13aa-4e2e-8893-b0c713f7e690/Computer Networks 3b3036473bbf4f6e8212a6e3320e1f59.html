THE OSI MODEL

ISO is the organization and OSI is the model

  • Exchange through OSI Model
    • Sender: Headers are added
    • Receiver: Headers are removed

  • 7 Layers of the OSI Model
    1. Physical

      Responsible for movement of individual bits from one hop (node) to the next

      To provide mechanical and electrical specifications

    1. Data Link

      Moving frames from on hop to the next

      To organize bits into frames and hop-to-hop delivery

      • Hop-to-Hop Delivery
    1. Network

      Delivery of individual packets from the source host to the destination host

      To provide internet working

      • Source-to-Destination Delivery
    1. Transport

      Delivery of a message from one process to another

      To provide reliable process to process message delivery and error recovery

      • Process-to-Process Delivery
    1. Session

      Dialog control and synchronization

      To establish, manage, and terminate sessions

    1. Presentation

      Translation, Compression & Encryption of the data

    1. Application

      Provides services to the User and allows access to network resources
